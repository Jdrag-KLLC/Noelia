The Challenges Families Face

Too often, children with special needs are left behind.

In regions like Bolivia, families often face this challenge alone, without the resources they need to support their loved ones. Children and adults with special needs face numerous barriers in education, healthcare, essential services, social exclusion, and career opportunities.

Noelia Foundation is headquartered in Fairfax, VA with active programs across Latin America, where over 19 million people live with special needs and access to care remains critically limited.

How Noelia Steps In

From isolation to opportunity.

Noelia is a U.S.-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to supporting disadvantaged communities throughout Latin America. By delivering essential services, connecting families to critical resources, and advocating for inclusion, we help children and young adults with special needs move from isolation to opportunity.

Intentionally designed to scale at a global level, our vast network includes experts in healthcare, education, and advocacy. As our network continues to grow, so does our impact.

Our Promise to You

Our Story

At Noelia, our mission goes beyond addressing immediate needs — we are working to transform what is possible for children and young adults with special needs and the families who support them.

Founder's Story

Founded on personal experience. Built for lasting change.

Osman Cuadros, founder of Noelia, established the organization to give back to children, young adults, and families who need support most. As a father of a child with special needs, Osman has a deeply personal understanding of the daily challenges these families face.

Noelia is more than a nonprofit — it is a lifeline, providing access to critical support systems that uplift and empower communities across Latin America.

Noelia Foundation is a registered U.S. 501(c)(3) nonprofit, headquartered in Fairfax, VA, and governed by a dedicated board of directors committed to financial transparency, responsible stewardship, and scalable impact.

01 — Access

Expanding Access to Essential Services

Our Programs

Where care meets community.

Our programs are designed to meet children with special needs and their families where they are — providing essential care, building local capacity, and creating pathways to long-term inclusion.

Program 01 — Therapeutic Services

Therapeutic Services

Access to consistent, high-quality therapy can change the trajectory of a child — but for many families, it remains out of reach.

Noelia brings speech and developmental therapies to the communities that need them most. These services focus on improving communication, motor skills, and cognitive development, helping children gain independence and confidence over time.

By prioritizing early and ongoing intervention, we support meaningful progress that extends far beyond the therapy setting and into everyday life.

Learn More →
Program 02 — Community Centers & Resource Support

Community Centers & Resource Support

Families often face overwhelming barriers when trying to access care, education, and basic support systems for loved ones with special needs. Noelia specializes in building, remodeling, and staffing community centers with various therapeutic services.

Currently, Noelia operates a community center in Santa Cruz, Bolivia, with a roadmap for replication of similar centers across underserved communities in Latin America.

In tandem with our community centers, we leverage local grassroots initiatives to connect families to essential services, guidance, and support networks within their communities.

Details on the Santa Cruz center coming soon.

Learn More →
Program 03 — Training & Capacity Building

Training & Capacity Building

Through our “train-the-trainer” model, Noelia equips educators, healthcare providers, and community leaders with the skills and knowledge needed to support children with special needs.

By investing in local professionals, we expand the reach of care and ensure that support continues long after direct services are delivered.

Build and Grow Project

Our goal is to build, remodel, and staff many more community centers dedicated to children and young adults with special needs throughout Latin America.
